Butterfly

They see a range of ultraviolet colours, invisible to the human eye

  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • Class: Insecta
  • Order: Lepidoptera
  • Suborder: Rhopalocera

They are beautiful flying animals that have unique characteristics that are unlike any other. They have two pairs of large wings covered with colorful, iridescent scales in overlapping rows. Butterflies can only fly if their body temperature is above 86 degrees.
